Abstract

I am an astronomer and aerospace engineer, studying asteroids and other rocky bodies. I completed my Bachelor of Science at MIT (February '24), studying Earth, Atmospheric, and Planetary Sciences and Engineering as Recommended by the Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics, with a concentration in Russian. My work at MIT has been primarily on asteroids in the Koronis family, which I have observed at the MIT Wallace Astrophysical Observatory as well as on multiple telescopes at Teide Observatory in Tenerife, Spain.
